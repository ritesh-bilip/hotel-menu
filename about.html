RK Restaurant Guidelines and Policies

## Introduction

Welcome to RK Restaurant! We are committed to providing you with an exceptional dining experience. Our goal is to create a welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy delicious meals prepared with the finest ingredients. To ensure a smooth operation and maintain high standards of service, we have outlined our guidelines and policies below.

## General Guidelines

1. **Opening Hours**

**Monday to Thursday:** 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

**Friday to Sunday:** 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM

**Public Holidays:** 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM

>2. **Reservation Policy**

Reservations can be made online through our website or by calling the restaurant directly.

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially for large parties.

Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the scheduled time. If you are running late, please inform us as soon as possible.

3. **Dress Code**

casual attire is recommended.

Offensive or inappropriate clothing is not permitted.

4. **Parking**

Complimentary parking is available for our guests.

Please adhere to the designated parking areas to avoid inconvenience.

## Customer Service Policies

1. **Seating**

We strive to accommodate all seating requests based on availability.

Special seating arrangements for events or celebrations can be requested in advance.

2. **Menu and Dietary Restrictions**

Our menu includes a variety of options, including vegetarian and gluten-free dishes.

Please inform your server of any food allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order.

3. **Service Charge and Gratuity**

A service charge of 10% is automatically added to tables of 8 or more.

Gratuity is not included for smaller parties but is appreciated.

4. **Complaints and Feedback**

If you have any concerns or feedback, please speak to a manager or contact us through our website.

We are committed to resolving any issues promptly and to your satisfaction.

## Safety and Hygiene Policies

1. **Health and Safety**

We adhere to all local health and safety regulations to ensure a clean and safe environment.

Our staff undergoes regular training in food safety and hygiene practices.

2. **COVID-19 Measures**

Hand sanitizers are available at entrances and throughout the restaurant.

We follow social distancing guidelines and enhanced cleaning protocols.

Masks are encouraged but not mandatory.

3. **Allergies and Special Requests**

Our kitchen staff is trained to handle allergen requests and cross-contamination issues.

Please inform us of any allergies or special dietary needs before ordering.

4. **Cleanliness**

Tables, chairs, and restrooms are cleaned and sanitized regularly.

We use environmentally friendly cleaning products.

## Code of Conduct

1. **Respectful Behavior**

We expect all guests to behave respectfully towards our staff and other patrons.

Any form of harassment, abusive language, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

2. **Smoking Policy**

Smoking is not permitted inside the restaurant.

Designated smoking areas are provided outside the building.

3. **Pets**

Pets are not allowed inside the restaurant, except for service animals.

Service animals must be under control and on a leash or harness at all times.

4. **Personal Belongings**

RK Restaurant is not responsible for lost or stolen personal items.

Please keep your belongings with you at all times.

## Events and Catering

1. **Private Events**

We offer private dining rooms for special occasions and events.

Please contact our event coordinator for availability and booking details.

2. **Catering Services**

RK Restaurant provides catering services for corporate events, parties, and other functions.

Customized menus and service options are available upon request.
